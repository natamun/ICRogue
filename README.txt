#How to play game
Run the Play class

# Game Description:
The objective of the game is to defeat the dark lord, the ruler of the dungeon.
Different items as well as a friendly NPC will help you on your quest.

Please find below the guide to our game:*

# Key Binds :
    - UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT : directional arrows
    - INTERACT/CLOSE DIALOG : E
    - SPRINT : W
    - USE WEAPON : Q
    - SWITCH WEAPON : S
    - PAUSE GAME : ESCAPE

(In the event you want to modify the keys, you can change them in the Interface "ICRogueKeyBinds".)

#Unfolding of the game

Find a grey key to access the turret room as well as a fire staff to defeat them.
Once the turrets defeated get a water staff and the red key to access the boss room.
Find the boss room and defeat him to finish the game !

#Weapons of the game
You will find two different weapons in this game. One is available before defeating the turrets, try to explore the
level to find it before going in the room containing the turrets. It is available in a room that does not require a
key to open.

# Behavior of turrets
They will shoot at you if they see you in their lane. Don't get to close as they also deal contact damage, you'll need
a staff to defeat them.


# How to defeat the dark lord:

The dark  lord will spawn flying flame skulls that will follow you around. However, you can't fight fire with fire,
try to find a water staff to defeat the flame skulls. You can defeat the boss using any weapon by shooting projectiles
but don't let him come to close as he could kill you through his deadly touch !

We hope you have a nice time trying out our game !!!

Note: the game is hard on purpose, to make it easier to defeat the boss you can increase the water ball damage.
